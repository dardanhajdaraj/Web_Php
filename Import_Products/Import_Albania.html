ALL THE THINGS WE EXPORT FROM ALBANIA

Minerals and Ores

Albania has significant deposits of chrome, copper, nickel, and other minerals, making mineral ores one of its major exports.

Agricultural Products

Albania exports a variety of agricultural products, including fruits (olives, citrus fruits), vegetables, and tobacco.

Textiles and Clothing

The textile and clothing industry is an important part of Albania, and these products we exporte to various countries.

Footwear

Albanian footwear, including shoes and other leather products, is exported to international markets.

Construction Materials

Building materials, including cement and construction aggregates, are exported from Albania.

Energy

Albania exports electricity to neighboring countries, and its hydropower potential contributes significantly to the energy sector.

Mineral Fuels

We exports from Albania petroleum and petroleum products.

Processed Food and Beverages

Processed food items, such as dairy products, meat products, and beverages, are exported from Albania.

Machinery and Equipment:

While the machinery and equipment sector is still developing, Albania does export machinery and equipment to some extent.

Plastics and Rubber Products

We exports from Albania various plastic and rubber products.
