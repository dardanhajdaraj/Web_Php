ALL THE THINGS WE EXPORT FROM KOSOVA

Minerals and Ores

Kosovo has significant mineral resources, including lead, zinc, and other metals, which contribute to its exports.

Agricultural Products

Kosovo exports agricultural products such as fruits (plums, cherries, and apples), vegetables, and processed foods.

Textiles and Clothing

The textile and apparel industry is a notable sector, with Kosovo exporting garments and textiles.

Wood and Wood Products

Kosovo exports wood and wood-based products.

Electrical and Electronic Equipment

Kosovo has been involved in the export of electrical and electronic goods.

Furniture

The furniture industry in Kosovo produces and exports various types of furniture.

Mineral Fuels

Kosovo exports coal, which is a significant natural resource in the region.

Processed Food and Beverages

Processed food items, including dairy products and beverages, are part of Kosovo's exports.

Machinery and Equipment:

While the machinery and equipment sector is still developing, Kosova does export machinery and equipment to some extent.

Plastics and Rubber Products

We exports from Kosova various plastic and rubber products.
