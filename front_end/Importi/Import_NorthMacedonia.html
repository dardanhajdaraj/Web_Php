ALL THE THINGS WE EXPORT FROM NORTH MACEDONIA

Automotive Parts

The automotive industry, including the production of automotive parts, contributes to North Macedonia's exports.

Agricultural Products

North Macedonia exports agricultural products such as fruits (grapes, apples, and plums), vegetables, and tobacco.

Textiles and Apparel

The textile and apparel industry is a significant contributor to North Macedonia's exports, with garments and textile products being shipped internationally.

Metals and Ores

North Macedonia exports non-ferrous metals, including copper and zinc, as well as other ores.

Electrical Machinery and Equipment

Electronic equipment and machinery are part of North Macedonia's export portfolio..

Furniture

The furniture industry in North Macedonia contributes to its exports, with various furniture products being shipped internationally.

Energy Products

North Macedonia exports electricity and related energy products.

Food and Beverages

Processed food items, including dairy products, meat products, and beverages, are part of North Macedonia's export profile.

Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals

North Macedonia exports chemicals and pharmaceutical products.

Plastics and Rubber Products

Various plastic and rubber products are exported from North Macedonia.
